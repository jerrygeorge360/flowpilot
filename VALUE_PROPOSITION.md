# FlowPilot Value Proposition

## Your money should run on autopilot.

**FlowPilot turns plain-English financial goals into on-chain automation.**
You say what you want. FlowPilot builds the plan, shows you exactly what will happen, and executes it on schedule after one confirmation.

No daily micromanagement. No custodial handoff. No black box.

---

## The promise

FlowPilot gives everyday users access to DeFi automation with the trust guarantees power users expect:

- **Simple to use** — type your goal in plain English
- **Safe by design** — Cadence resource model protects asset handling
- **Self-custodial** — you keep control of your wallet and funds
- **Always-on execution** — Flow’s native scheduler handles recurrence on-chain
- **Private intent parsing** — NEAR AI infrastructure supports privacy-conscious inference

---

## What you can automate

With one confirmation, FlowPilot can:

- schedule recurring saves
- route idle balance into yield strategies
- execute recurring DCA-style actions
- keep funds accessible for withdrawal anytime

Every rule is visible and auditable on-chain.

---

## How it works

1. **Describe the outcome**
    “Save 50 FLOW weekly and earn yield on idle funds.”

2. **Review the execution plan**
    FlowPilot shows a clear human-readable preview before activation.

3. **Confirm once**
    The rule is stored on-chain and runs automatically on schedule.

Most products force a tradeoff between usability and control.
FlowPilot removes that tradeoff.

- Compared with manual DeFi: less effort, same ownership
- Compared with custodial apps: similar convenience, stronger user control
- Compared with off-chain keeper systems: native on-chain scheduling

FlowPilot is built for people who want modern UX without giving up transparency or custody.
